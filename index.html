Welcome toChalé Hotel,
the refuge done for you!

The Chalé hotel chain is among the most luxurious and cozy hotels in Brazil. Well, we offer excellent accommodation for business and leisure travelers.

The most famous unit is in Copacabana, a city located in the South Zone of Rio de Janeiro. The hotel has 200 rooms (including 50 PetFriendly rooms) with unique details like vaulted ceilings, exposed beams, and exquisite wallpaper.

We have units in: São Paulo - Itaim Bibi; Rio de Janeiro - Copacabana and Belo Horizonte - Ouro Preto
